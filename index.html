About Me

I have always had a passion for technology. Throughout my life, I have been constantly tinkering with, building, and fixing electronics. I studied Computer Science at the University of Central Florida to pursue my passion for technology. For the last year I have been providing tech support for Verizon Wireless customers. I obtained my A+ Certification from CompTIA and I am continuously furthering my education. I have studied network tools and infrastructure, web development, and cybersecurity. I actually built this website from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Check out my portfolio to see other projects that I have been working on lately!

While studying at the university, I encountered many new people and ideas and was introduced to a new passion: yoga and movement arts. Shortly thereafter I witnessed my first fire dancing performance, and I was immediately mesmerized. After years of practice, I started my first small business: Phoenix Fire & Performance Art, and I became a certified Yoga Instructor. At Phoenix Fire, I led a troupe of performers who specialized in fire dancing, acrobatics, juggling, LED light shows, fire eating, and fire breathing! As my business developed, I realized that myself and many others had a need for higher quality tools to be used for training and performing. This is what led me to start my second business: Liquid Flow Props. At Liquid Flow I designed, manufactured, sold, and repaired high quality fire performance tools. After 6 insightful years, I shut down Phoenix Fire and Liquid Flow in order to pursue my career in IT. Though that may have been the end of a chapter, a Phoenix never really dies does it? Below, you can check out my websites for both of these startups to learn more.
